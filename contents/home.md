
**Lei Han** holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Nanyang Technological University (NTU)](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/).

His research focuses on **reasoning architectures for large language models (LLMs)**, with particular emphasis on **Chain-of-Thought (CoT)** and **Graph of Thoughts (GoT)**.

#### Education

Ph.D., Computer Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

#### Research Interests

Large language models; reasoning and planning; Chain-of-Thought (CoT); Graph of Thoughts (GoT).
